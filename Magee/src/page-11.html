Enjoy Refined Décor and Superior Amenities

Accommodations at Magee Homestead are found in a collection of nine historically restored luxury cabin residences and suites, beautifully decorated by celebrated interiors firm Simeone Deary Design Group, with 12 total bedrooms for a maximum of 27 guests. A "Rent the Homestead" reservation of the entire property allows complete exclusivity and the opportunity for children to experience this otherwise adult-only collection of luxury cabins and suites in Wyoming.

Ideal for intimate group gatherings, the two luxury cabin residences feature a two- or three-bedroom layout with kitchen facilities, a spacious living area, and two or three bathrooms. Meanwhile, the restored luxury cabin suites offer the perfect romantic cottage setting, ideal for couples enjoying a honeymoon, anniversary, or vow renewal. Some cabin suites are equipped with a private fireplace and a creek-side bistro deck.

TZ DELUXE CABIN RESIDENCE
Unique design details in every room

The TZ Deluxe Cabin Residence is the largest of the cabins at the Magee Homestead at 1,929 square feet, featuring three bedrooms, three bathrooms, a full kitchen and open living and dining area. This one story, hand-hewn log cabin, located adjacent to the Wellness Pavilion, boasts high wood beam ceilings and a grand stone gas fireplace, perfect to curl up beside with a good book from the cabin’s large library collection. The natural architectural elements of wood and stone are contrasted throughout the cabin by luxurious, contemporary rustic furnishings and decor, rich in color and texture. You will find yourself surprised and delighted by the unique design details in every room of this spectacular abode, as well as drawn to the views of the calming pastoral landscapes that surround you.

The kitchen, with its eye catching vintage butcher’s block, is fully equipped with all the essentials, including stainless steel appliances, a fridge, dishwasher, and oven. The grand dining table seats eight people and the dining room’s large southwest facing windows boast stunning landscape views.

The living area features a leather queen sleeper sofa, two armchairs, a huge rustic coffee table, and a grand stone gas fireplace surrounded by ceiling-high shelves full of books. The master bedroom has a king sized bed, private bathroom, closet and chest of drawers. French doors open up to the cabin’s partial wrap around deck. The second bedroom, decorated in warm red tones, has a queen sized bed, private bathroom, and closet. The third bedroom features a queen sized bed, private bathroom, and a large wooden armoire for clothing storage.

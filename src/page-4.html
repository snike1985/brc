As a member of our elite team, there is plenty to look forward to:

  • Live in the heart of the American West and experience the rare opportunity to live and work on  a historic cattle ranch.
  • Gain valuable and unique employment and leadership experience while building your career with White Lodging, one of the hospitality industry's most recognized leaders.
  • Enjoy competitive compensation which includes: a monthly salary, housing, meals, and an end of season service gratuity.
  • Explore and discover all the activities that Brush Creek Ranch offers across the ranch's 30,000 acres, along with the surrounding Platte River Valley and the majestic Medicine Bow National Forest.
  • Connect with other like-minded individuals and forge new friendships as you work together to ensure the highest level of service and fulfill our goal of creating lifetime guests at Brush Creek Ranch.

*NOTE: We will primarily consider applicants who are able to work the duration of the summer season, from May through October.

*HOUSING: Our housing provided for seasonal positions consists of clean, modern, dormitory-style rooms with up to 4 people per room. Our seasonal housing is not suitable for families or pets; however, the nearby towns of Saratoga, Encampment, and Riverside often have properties available for seasonal rental.
